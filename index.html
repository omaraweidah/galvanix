Introduction to GALVANIX

Galvanix is the world's first and only manufacturer of 100mm foam-filled interlocking steel slats, setting new standards for industrial and commercial shutter security. We pair advanced engineering with premium raw materials to deliver durable, insulated, and custom-fit solutions across the region.

Our patented interlocking system provides superior security and weather sealing, while our hot-dip galvanized steel with 120 zinc coating ensures maximum durability in extreme climates.
